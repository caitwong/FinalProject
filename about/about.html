Here at BigFoot, we value one thing: Ensuring all feet find their perfect shoe.


Once upon a time, there was a girl named Skyler who had wide feet. Her family searched far and wide for Skyler's perfect shoe, but none seemed to be the Cinderella glass slipper. Skyler's shoes options began to dwindle and her family doubted they would ever find the one.

Until one day, her older sister had a brilliant idea! Instead of hunting for this nonexistent "glass slipper", what if they created their own? And so BigFoot was born!

Now all who have wide feet in need of finding inexpensive, good quality, fitting shoes, can find just that! To all the girls and boys, men and women, whose feet never fit Cinderella's glass slipper, never give up hope. BigFoot has the shoe for you!
